Santorini, Greece

Santorinii is possibly one of the most famous islands in the Aegean sea, with its beautiful white and blue domed architecture and its breathtaking views. The amazing geography of Satorini allows for the clearest of beaches and stunning views, sunsets in Santorini are unmatched.
